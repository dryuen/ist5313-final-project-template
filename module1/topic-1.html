Module 1: Reducing Debt

Summary of Course

Being in debt is when you have something owed to someone. In this case, the debt we are talking about is when money is owed to someone. According to Pew Research in 2015, 80% of Americans are financially in debt. The Median Debt owed by Americans is about $67,900 and the average American in debt is usually associated with mortgage debt.
