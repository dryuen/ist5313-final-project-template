Budgeting Plan of Action

Our next step is determine how much money you earn for each month, and how much of that will be used for essential expenses and towards your debts.

Round up the following most recent bills:

  • Car payments
  • Rent or house payments
  • Electricity payment
  • Water payment
  • Trash/Waste payment
  • Cell-Phone payments
  • Internet/cable payments
  • Credit card payments
  • Extra subscriptions
  • Loans
  • Insurance payments

More importantly, download your bank statements from your bank account to calculate how much money you spend on day-to-day living expenses like groceries, gas transportation, and leisure activities like eating out or going to the movies.
