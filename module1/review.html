Remember Lucy? Let’s re-examine her situation to see if we can help. Let’s start by writing equations for each of the phone plans. Since the Walk & Talk company offers a $50 base charge and $10 per GB of data their equation would be y = 10x + 50. Phonetronic offers a $75 base charge and $5 per GB of data, so their equation would be y = 5x + 75. Let’s fill up the table using this information.

(Tables fill in.)

From the table we can see that the cost of each plan would be the same with 5 GB of data. So which company should Lucy choose? If she wants less than 5 GB of data she should go with the plan from Walk & Talk, if she wants more than 5 GB of data then she should choose the plan from Phonetronic, and if she wants exactly 5 GB of data then she can choose either plan and spend the same amount of money.
