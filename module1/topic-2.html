For Fine Wine Sales Representatives who are motivated to provide an inviting atmosphere and a competitive edge selling to and off premise business the WineTech Training System is an online short course that provides consultants with key information about proper ways to market wine. The WineTech training system offers videos and end of module tests to ensure knowledge of how to properly market wine, unlike ordinary training booklets or pamphlets, our product provides a detailed history of wine and how it is made, the do’s and don’ts of hosting an at-home wine party, and a chance to create their own business selling wine.
