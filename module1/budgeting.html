Budgeting 101

By completing this section you will learn:

  • A clear understanding of exactly how much money you spend and how much you owe
  • New ways to reduce future spending that may lead to debt
  • Money saving techniques
  • A step towards minimalistic spending and lifestyle

Before continuing please take the time to print and fill out this Debt Spreadsheet.

Begin by listing your personal debts owed to creditors and family members if applicable, then write the amount owed to the creditor. If it applies, write the monthly minimum as well as the annual credit rate for each creditor.

Take your time when filling this sheet out and be honest to yourself. If there is any single debt, no matter how small or big, write it down. This is the start of something much larger. It’s a change of personality, and the path towards financial peace! The beginning of this process is not easy. As you may find, this learning course, specifically this module, will be difficult to begin and will require great amounts of work and effort. But it gets easier.
