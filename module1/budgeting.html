Budgeting 101

Ways to reduce monthly spending.

  • plan your monthly spending
  • Identify what percentage will be needed to pay utilities, rent, gas, and groceries
  • Cord cutting: Use cheaper methods of watching entertainment like Hulu or Netflix.
  • Buy expensive groceries in Bulk
  • Reduce eating out to once a week or once every two weeks.
  • Set a spending limit on entertainment
  • Save a percentage of you monthly checks
  • Make coffee at home instead of buying it
  • Start a side hustle
  • Sell belongings that have been sitting around
