12-12-12 Challenge

A simple way to help declutter your home is to participate in the 12-12-12 challenge. Locate the following:

12 items to throw away
Search through places that you often overlook, such as your garage or closet.
12 items to donate
What are some things that you are no longer using?
12 items to be returned to their proper home
Tasks as simple as placing a cup on the counter into the cupboard make a difference.
