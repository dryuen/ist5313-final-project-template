Capsule Wardrobe

A capsule wardrobe allows you to:

  • Spend less time worrying about what to wear
  • Be creative with your outfits
  • Control your spending on clothes
  • Wear what you love everyday

Follow the three S's to start your journey

NEED A DIFFERENT BACKGROUND IMAGE HERE!

1. Survey/Scan Lay out ALL of your clothing, shoes, jewelry, and accessories. Seeing everything at once makes it easier to admit how much “stuff” you’ve acquired! Look at all the things you forgot you ever bought (with tags!) and things you’re embarrassed you still have.

2. Sort Create four piles: Love, Like, Lose, Trash

3. SelectCreate a base wardrobe. This will not count as part of your capsule wardrobe because they are essentials (sleepwear, outerwear, work out clothes, undershirts for layering). Store this in a different area than your capsule wardrobe, like in your drawers.
