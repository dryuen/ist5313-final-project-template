Capsule Wardrobe

Many of us store clothing in our closets that we barely touch or forget was even in there! In an effort to make more space in our closets and take away the stress of figuring out what to wear the next day, we will choose essential pieces of clothing to create a variety of looks.

1. Lay out all of your clothes. Yes, all of them!
2. Divide them into piles: Love, Like, Can Do Without. Place the "Can Do Without" pile into a box to donate.
3. Take out the clothing that you will wear no matter the season (work out clothes, tanks and tees for layering, etc.)
4. Identify 3 categories for different occasions you'll need to be prepared for. Mine would be work, home, and date night.
5. Based on the current season, choose 9 tops, 5 bottoms, and 5 shoes that fit in one of the above categories. Make sure there is a balance!
