Let’s find out who won that race! If Jennifer has a head start of 15 yards and runs 3 yards per second her equation would be y=3x+15. If Lee begins at the endzone and runs 4 yards per second his equation would be y=4x+0 or just y=4x. Let’s graph each equation to find out if Lee catches up to Jennifer. (Graph the equations). The two lines intersect at (15,60). This means that Lee and Jennifer will both be at the 60 yard line after 15 seconds. And since Lee is running faster he will win the race.
