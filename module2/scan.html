Step 1: Scan

Lay out ALL of your clothing, shoes, jewelry, and accessories. Seeing everything at once makes it easier to admit how much “stuff” you’ve acquired! Look at all the things you forgot you ever bought (with tags!) and things you’re embarrassed you still have.
