Module 3: Improving Your Health

Healthy Lifestyle is a way of living that lowers the risk of being seriously ill or dying early, is one which helps to keep and improve people's health and well-being.Health is not only about avoiding disease. It is also about physical, mental and social well being. The ways to being healthy include healthy eating, physical activities, weight management, and stress management.
