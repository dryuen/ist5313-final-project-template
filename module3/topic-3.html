In the United States, there was an attempt to eliminate the drinking of alcohol during the years 1919 to 1933 by means of a national prohibition on sales and the manufacture of it.

Hence the name of the Prohibition Era though this lead to widespread procurement of alcohol by illegal means. A lucrative business was created for producers and sellers which then lead to the major development of organized crime.

Prohibition was soon repealed by the 21st Amendment to the Constitution.
