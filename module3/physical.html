Physical Health

Minimalism provides myriad benefits. Less Stress. More time. A greater appreciation for the people and events that truly matter in your life. Follow these bare essentials of Minimalist Guide to Physical Health will improve your health while saving precious time and even money.
  1. Move Your Body in a Way You Enjoy
  2. Take advantage of Local Parks
  3. Go Hiking
  4. Ride a Bike
  5. Push-up
  6. Pull-up
  7. Squats
