Mental Health

Living with Less means more mental health. But how do you mentally declutter? In short, clear your mind of the obsessive thoughts which cause overwhelm and keep you mired in avoidance, indecision and procrastination. While no shortcuts exist to get rid of unwanted thoughts, the following minimalist mindsets can help set the tone for a cleaner psychological slate.
