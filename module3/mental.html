Mental Health

Minimalism cherishes the concepts of simplicity and necessity which can be applied to mental health. Living with less, frequently practicing mental decluttering and slowing down your pace are great tips to maintain mental health.

Living with Less
Living with less can lead to an organized household and money in savings, but there are also a few deep, life-changing benefits such as creating a calm, peaceful mind.
