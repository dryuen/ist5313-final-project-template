Types of Storage

 
            
     

Audio


Captions

There are many ways to store wine from cabinets to fridges, wooden wine racks, and even cellar units.

When storing red wine keep its best to keep it out of the light and at room temperature especially if the bottle has been opened; this is so because once the wine has been opened the oxygen turns the wine into vinegar.

Storing the wine in a fridge at a temperature between 50 and 59 degrees fahrenheit is best practice.
