Overview

This course draws content from a variety of fields, including psychology, neuroscience, and behavior, to help you develop a MinimallyU lifestyle.

Objectives

Our course will challenge your inner self to identify important aspects of your life and do more with less financial stress, less possessions, and less toxic food. Through purposeful, intentional actions, our program helps to individually develop a new minimallyU.
