Food Pairings





Audio


Captions

Bolder red wines like Cabernet and Syrah/Shiraz go well with red and cured meats such as steak and sharp cheeses like Gouda. Pinot Noir can be served well with chicken and fish like crab and roasted vegetables. As far as white wines the buttery Chardonnay can be paired with white meat and fish, like turkey and lobster. The drier whites, Sauvignon Blanc and Pinot Grigio, can be coupled with green vegetables, like asparagus, fish, and carbs, like potatoes. For those with a sweet tooth dessert wine like port can be served with dark or milk chocolate, fruit, and baked goods.
