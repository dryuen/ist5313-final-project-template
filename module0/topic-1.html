Summary of Course

MinimallyU focuses on the practices of minimalism, a life where you are no longer bogged down by worrying about everyday finances, useless things purchased through impulsive shopping or poor eating habits that make you sick. We help you understand the benefits of minimalism to assist in gaining a higher-quality of life with purpose.
