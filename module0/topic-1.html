Module 0: Welcome to MinimallyU

Summary of Course

How might your life be better with less stuff? How are your day to day finances impacting your quality of life? Are you feeling overwhelmed by possessions? Do you stress eat? Ask yourself what you can do with a simpler healthier debt free lifestyle. MinimiallyU helps to support one's individual goals where you are not bogged down by worrying about everyday finances, useless stuff through impulsive shopping and poor eating habits that make you sick. We help you to understand the benefits of minimalism to assist you in gaining a higher-quality of life's purpose.

VIDEO HERE: Minimalism can be defined by simplifying one’s lifestyle by removing objects that distract individuals from their true life’s purpose. At its core, minimalism is the intentional promotion of the things we most value and the removal of everything that distracts us from it. Minimalism is not a journey’s end; instead, it’s a way of living—a set of guiding principles that help us make decisions in our everyday lives. Minimalism is a paradigm shift that can happen at any stage of life, for any purpose and for any person.
